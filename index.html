Based on the synergies of sustainability, social and governance, we aim to bridge the gap between awareness and action for purposeful leadership by educating students and professionals on how to ally business strategy to engagement and leader business responsibly in a increasingly more complex environment.

NetPositive is an HEC Paris student association, founded in October 2021. We do currently consist of over 70 members with more than 30 nationalities and work in close collaboration with the whol HEC Paris community.

If you have any questions or are interested in becoming a sponsor for our project, please do not hesitate to contact us at mark-justus.burmeister@hec.edu.
